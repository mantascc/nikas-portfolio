## Design direction
- **Visual style:** Clean & minimal — lots of whitespace, restrained palette
- **Structure:** Single long-scroll page with project sections (no separate sub-pages)
- **Tone:** Editorial, thoughtful, systems-oriented — avoid generic "AI aesthetic" or template feel
- **Imagery:** Project files (screenshots of My Fleet, Mission Control, etc.) are available in the project and will be used as case study visuals
