My Fleet

customer support platform rebuild · SITA
Role
Lead designer.
Challenge
Refactor the customer support platform, expand self-service capabilities, and widen product coverage — without breaking the paths existing customers already knew.
Contribution
Led the rebuild across information architecture, visual style, and usability. Laid the foundation for rapid functional expansion so subsequent teams could ship features against a consistent spine.
Outcome
Platform rebuilt end-to-end. Shipped ticket management, file and training libraries, service status, a home dashboard, and more — on the new foundation.

2024

Mission Control

aviation ms teams plug-in · SITA
Role
Lead designer.
Challenge
Find a new direction for an MS Teams plug-in offering flight-contextual communication for airline teams. The existing value proposition wasn't landing.
Contribution
Led research and discovery with current and prospective clients; redefined the proposition around OCC and pilot workflows. Reframed the product around right information, right time rather than generic messaging.
Outcome
Two work streams defined — pilot (pre-flight & in-flight collaboration) and OCC (flight prep & monitoring). Project ultimately cancelled to consolidate teams on flagship products; the discovery artefacts outlived the product.

Project
2025

Opti Flight

tactical flight trajectory optimisation · SITA / eWAS+
Role
Designer leading discovery.
Challenge
Bring an ML-driven fuel-saving recommendation engine into an iPad map app (eWAS+) that already hosts weather-awareness tools. Recommendations can also reach pilots via ACARS. Two co-habiting data tools, one shared map.
Contribution
Led discovery on what it means for this solution to coexist with weather data in a shared map space. Ran internal workshops, created design artefacts, conducted early pilot interviews, and defined a roadmap of epics. Coordinated two product teams on roadmap execution and workflow synergy.
Outcome
Discovery concluded with a prioritised roadmap. Follow-up research phase launched to validate features and run V1 usability testing.
